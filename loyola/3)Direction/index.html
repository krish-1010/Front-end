Step By Step Directions To Reach Physics Department From Liba Gate

  • From the Liba Gate walk towards the Men's Hostel Gate.
  • Enter the Men's Hostel Gate from one side and exit it from the other side.
  • Walk straight towards the Jubliee building where you will find Vice Principals office.
  • Adjacent to the Office will be stairs
  • Climb up the stairs, you will find UG Physics Department in the first floor.
